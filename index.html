Our Services

From logbook servicing to full mechanical repairs, our qualified technicians handle it all with precision and care.

🔧 01

Logbook Servicing

Manufacturer-approved servicing that maintains your warranty. We follow your car's specific logbook requirements to keep it running at peak performance.

🛞 02

Brake & Suspension

Full inspection, repair and replacement of brakes, shock absorbers, springs and steering components. Your safety is our top priority.

⚙️ 03

Engine Diagnostics

Advanced computer diagnostics to identify and resolve engine faults, warning lights and performance issues quickly and accurately.

❄️ 04

Air Conditioning

AC regas, repair and servicing to keep you cool all year round. We service all makes and models with genuine refrigerant.

🔋 05

Electrical Systems

Battery testing, alternator repair, wiring faults and electrical diagnostics on all vehicle types including European and Japanese makes.

🚗 06

Roadworthy Certificates

Licensed vehicle testers providing VIC roadworthy certificates. Fast turnaround, transparent inspection and competitive pricing.

🔩 07

Transmission & Clutch

Automatic and manual transmission servicing, clutch replacement, gearbox repairs and fluid flushes across all makes and models.

Exhaust & Muffler

Full exhaust system inspection, catalytic converter checks, muffler replacement and custom exhaust work. Reduce emissions, improve performance.

Sunshine Road Motors has been proudly serving the Tottenham and western Melbourne community for over 15 years. Founded on the principles of honest service and quality workmanship, we've built our reputation one satisfied customer at a time.
